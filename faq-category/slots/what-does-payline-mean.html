    • What does Payline mean?

    The number of paylines available in a game is among the most important features of any slot machine. A payline, in simple terms, is the line on which a payout will be awarded based on winning combinations. In general, the more lines you play, the more chances you have to win. But the more lines you play, the more each spin is cost.

    To be sure of all possible wins and payouts, always view the paytable, usually located within the main gaming screen. Here you will find paylines, payout odds, winning combos, and more, including info on how to play the bonus events.
